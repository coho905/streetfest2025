StreetFest 2025

July 19 • Phi Delta Alpha

About Our Event

Created during the Class of 2025's sophomore summer, StreetFest celebrates Dartmouth's sophomore performance groups while giving back to the Upper Valley. Each year, proceeds support a charitable cause selected by the house. This year, we're supporting the Upper Valley Haven!

A Full Day of Fun & Fundraising

Enjoy interactive activities hosted by Dartmouth Greek organizations — games, live performances, and family-friendly booths — all supporting Upper Valley Haven.

Benefiting Upper Valley Haven

Upper Valley Haven provides food, shelter, and critical resources to individuals and families overcoming poverty across the region.
Every dollar raised at StreetFest goes directly to these services!

Donate Now

We invite friends and families of the Dartmouth community and members of the Hanover community to join us in support!
